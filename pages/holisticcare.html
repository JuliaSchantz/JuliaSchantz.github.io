Holistic Care

There are over 17,000 children in the Ohio foster care system.

The key to success after leaving the system is holistic care.

Holistic care from individuals who are committed and love like Christ changes everything.

  • Spiritual and physical care help build a foundation for youth to grow.

  • Emotional care acknowledges trauma, helps the healing process begin, and helps establish healthy coping mechanisms.

  • Education provides a sense of dignity and aids in breaking the cycle of poverty and crime.

  • Social skills grow confidence to intigrate into society and pursue beneficial community.
