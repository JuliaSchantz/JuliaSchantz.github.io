What We Do

Through our mentorship program, we help youth change their trajectory and break the foster care cycle. We are a non-profit organization based in Ohio, so our program is specifically targeted toward the Ohio foster care system.

Our organization prioritized holistic care. We partner with other non-profit organizations that meet physical and emotional needs, so we can focus on spiritual and social needs.

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

Our main ministry is our mentorship program. This program partners church members with youth aging out of foster care. Our goal is for trust to be built so the mentor relationship can be a safe place where the mentee feels seen, heard, and supported. The ultimate goal of mentoring is for youth going through the program to hear the gospel and have a smoother transition into society.

Mentors will also teach their youth basic life skills, like how to write a resume, how to dress for a job interview…etc. We want youth to be as equipped as possible for success and to have a place to get all their questions answered.

PROGRAM GOALS

  • Involve the church in cultivating a support system for post-foster care youth, so they are more equipt to transition out of the system

  • Give post-foster care youth a mentor relationship where they feel seen and can build trust.

  • Educate the church on the needs of foster care youth and provide opportunities beyond fostering or adopting.
