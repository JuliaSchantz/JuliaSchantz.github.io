Other Opportunities

Looking to serve but unable to mentor?

Check out other opportunities to get involved with our ministry! We are always in need of individuals who are passionate about serving those in or transitioning out of the foster care system. Help us care for the least of these.

  • PRAY

    We believe in the power of prayer. If you can’t help mentor, help cover youth leaving the foster care system and our ministry in prayer. Here is a list of specific requests from our team!

    • Hearts to be softened toward the gospel and trust can be built

    • Provision and safety for those aging out of foster care

    • We would have the ability to meet needs and show the love of Christ

    • Mentors would have strength and wisdom as they pour into our youth

  • VOLUNTEER

    Our ministry wouldn’t be possible without volunteers. We are always looking for passionate individuals who feel called to work behind the scenes to keep our ministry running. Below is a list of opportunities:

    • Fundraising

    • Sorting Donations

    • Tutoring

    • Meal Delivery

  • TRAIN

    Are you an expert at writing resumes, budgeting, or other necessary life skills? Consider training our mentors so they can teach their mentees skills to succeed. Below are a list of skills:

    • Car Maintenance

    • Resume Writing

    • Interview Skills

    • Cooking Basics

    • Filing Taxes
