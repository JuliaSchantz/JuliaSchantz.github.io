We Strive Together

Our goal is to get the church involved with foster care youth.

There is a lack of involvement with the foster care system that we believe mainly stems from a lack of knowledge on need or where to start. We strive to educate the church on foster care, encouraging a passion especially for youth leaving the system’s support. Although fostering children is a huge need that the church should be involved in meeting, we want to create other opportunities for individuals to get involved.
